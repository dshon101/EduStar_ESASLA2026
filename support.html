🎧 Support Center

Get help, check your ticket status, or browse common answers below.

🔑

I forgot my password

Go to Settings → Account & Security → Change Password. You'll need your current password. If locked out, submit a support ticket.

📚

A book link isn't working

Some books link to external sites (Siyavula, OpenStax). If a link is broken, please submit a ticket with the book name and we'll fix it.

🎓

My progress isn't saving

Make sure you're logged in. Progress saves to the server automatically. If problems persist, check your internet connection and try again.

🌍

My country isn't listed

We currently support 13 African countries. More are being added regularly. Let us know your country via a support ticket and we'll prioritise it.

🎨

How do I change the theme?

Go to Settings → Appearance. Choose from Dark, Midnight, Forest, Sunset, or Ocean. The theme applies to all pages instantly.

🗑️

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. You'll need to confirm with your password. This is permanent and cannot be undone.

💬

Community rules

Be respectful and helpful. No spam, no sharing personal information, stay on academic topics. Posts violating rules will be removed.

📧

Why am I getting login emails?

We only send an email when a new device or browser logs into your account. No IP address is included. You can turn this off in Settings → Notifications.
